KURUKU SOLL ( CROSSWORD )

Crossword puzzle Game.

KURUKU SOLL is an interesting and mind refreshing game
in which set of questions would be given in a particular theme and
the participants are supposed to fill the grid with appropriate answer
with respective to the questions given.

Rules and Regulation

  • NOTE: * REGISTRATION LINK WILL BE CLOSED EXACTLY 12/06/2022 - 10:00 PM
  • * On spot registrations are strictly not allowed.
  • * Participants are allowed to participate only in one team.
  • * Multiple registration of same name in different teams are subjected to direct disqualify.

  • 1. Maximum 2 members per team. A word will have minimum of three letters.
  • 2. Word cannot be formed diagonally.
  • 3. Word can be formed either horizontally or vertically.
  • 4. Participants should fill grid with respective to the question given within the stipulated time.
  • 5. The time limit for completing the crossword will be 30 minutes.
  • 6. Decisions taken by the judges will be final.
